Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built using **Power BI** to analyze Superstore sales data and provide actionable business insights for executive decision-making.

Project Overview

This project analyzes four years of Superstore sales data to evaluate business performance across sales, profitability, products, customers, regions, and operations.
The dashboard is designed for different stakeholders including executives, product managers, sales managers, and operations teams.

Rather than creating a single dashboard, the solution is divided into **four interactive dashboards**, each focusing on a specific business area.

Business Objectives

The project answers the following business questions:

- Is the company making a profit?
- Which products generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which products are underperforming?
- Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
- Which regions and states drive business growth?
- Which shipping method is used the most?
- How do discounts affect profitability?
- How do sales change over months, quarters, and years?

Dashboard Pages

1.Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.
 KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin

Visuals
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Orders by Ship Mode

2.Product Performance Dashboard
Analyzes product performance and profitability.
KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Products
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin

Visuals
- Sales by Sub-Category
- Top 5 Products by Sales
- Bottom 5 Products by Profit
- Profit by Category
- Sales vs Profit Scatter Plot

3.Customer & Geography Dashboard
Analyzes customer behavior and geographical performance.
KPIs
- Average Order Value
- Total Sales
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Profit

Visuals
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Top Cities by Sales
- Top States by Sales
- Top States by Profit

4.Operations & Performance Dashboard
 Analyzes operational efficiency and business trends.
KPIs
- Total Quantity
- Average Discount
- Average Delivery Days
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin

Visuals
- Orders by Ship Mode
- Average Delivery Days by Ship Mode
- Profit by Discount
- Quarterly Sales
- Monthly Sales Trend (Year-wise)

Key Business Insights
- Technology generated the highest overall profit.
- Furniture generated strong sales but comparatively lower profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the highest share of revenue.
- West region delivered the highest sales and profit.
- Standard Class shipping handled the majority of orders.
- Higher discounts were generally associated with lower average profit.
- Sales peaked during Q4, indicating strong seasonal demand.

Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing Technology products.
- Review pricing and discount strategy for Furniture.
- Expand marketing efforts in lower-performing regions.
- Optimize shipping methods to reduce delivery time.
- Monitor high-discount products to improve profitability.
- Plan inventory ahead of Q4 to meet seasonal demand.
